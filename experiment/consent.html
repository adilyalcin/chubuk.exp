Informed Consent for Research - Crowdsourced Evalation of Information Visualization

search The Research - Purpose of the Study

We invite you to participate in our research study that aims to understand how we perceive large amounts of information, and how chart design influences our perception.

check_circle You are eligible if:

looks_one You have not participated in this study before.
language You are fluent in English.
location_on You are located in the U.S.
people You are at least 18 years old.
computer You are using a laptop/desktop computer, not a mobile device.
palette You do not have color blindness or color vision deficiency.
poll You are comfortable with interpreting charts and graphs.

equalizer What will you do?

You will solve questions about fictional data using a number of charts to assist you in making decisions.
You will receive the necessary instructions when you start your participation.

timelapse How long will the study take?

We expect this study to take 5 to 15 minutes, with a more precise duration given in Amazon Mechanical Turk HIT.

wb_sunny What are your benefits?

This research is not designed to benefit you personally.
We hope that, in the future, other people might benefit from this study through improved information visualization design.

attach_money What is your compensation?

You will be paid with the targeted rate of $8 per hour upon successful completion of the experiment.
For example, the payment of an expected 5-minute long work is $0.70, 10-minute work is $1.35, and 15-minute work is $2.00.
Upon completion of the experiment, you will be given the code to claim your compensation on Amazon Mechanical Turk.
Your answers may be reviewed to avoid fraudulent and spam answers.

trending_up What are your risks?

We don't anticipate any risks from participation in this study greater than normal activity.
We discuss confidentiality of your participation below.

live_help How can you contact us?

For questions about this research, you can contact the researchers M. Adil Yalcin (email yalcin), or Niklas Elmqvist (email elm). If you are not satisfied with response of the research team, have more questions, or want to talk with someone about your rights as a research participant, you can contact University of Maryland College Park Institutional Review Board Office, 1204 Marie Mount Hall, College Park, Maryland, 20742 - mail irb@umd.edu - phone 301-405-0678.

thumb_up How do you consent?

Your participation in this study is voluntary.
You can withdraw from the study any time without penalty.
If you withdraw prior to completing the study, you will not receive compensation.

verified_user Data Confidentiality

Other than your user ID on Amazon Mechanical Turk, we will not collect any personal information from you.
Your answers in this study will be recorded and studied by researchers of this study.
Any personally identifable information will be kept confidential, and accessible only by the researchers.
In publications of this research, shared data will be limited to the answers submitted to perceptual tasks, and basic demographic information, if collected.

info_outline How is the study run?

Do not close or refresh this page during the study. If you do refresh it, you may lose your progress and become ineligible for this study.
When you click on the "Start" button, the webpage will cover your entire screen. Please allow this behavior. If the page is not full-screen, please enable full-screen mode of your browser. (F11 on Windows, ⇧⌘F on Mac OS)
